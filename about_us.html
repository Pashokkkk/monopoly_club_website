About Us

What is the MTU Monopoly Club?

The MTU Monopoly Club is a community of board game enthusiasts based at the MTU Bishopstown Campus. Our mission is to bring students together through their shared love for the classic board game Monopoly. Whether you're new to the game or a seasoned pro, the club offers a welcoming environment to connect, compete, and have fun!

Why Join the MTU Monopoly Club?

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can join the MTU Monopoly Club?
All MTU students are welcome to join, regardless of their skill level in Monopoly.
2. How can I sign up for events?
You can sign up for events through our Sign Up Page. Simply fill out the form and select the events you're interested in.
3. Are there any fees to join the club?
No, joining the MTU Monopoly Club is completely free!
